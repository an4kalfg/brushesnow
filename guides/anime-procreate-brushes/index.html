Anime Procreate Brushes for Character Art

Brushes and stamp sets for anime faces, hair, poses, manga textures, and expressive stylized illustration.

Browse related categoryView all guides

Curated brush picks

Anime Procreate brushes are most useful when they support the actual workflow of character art: rough sketching, hair design, pose planning, and stylized rendering. This guide focuses on tools that fit anime, kawaii, and manga-inspired drawing styles.

Best anime brush types to combine

A practical anime setup often includes one hair brush pack, one pose or stamp set, one manga texture pack, and one clean sketch or inking brush. Together they cover both the planning stage and the final rendering stage.

How to build a faster character workflow

Use stamps or pose brushes for layout, sketchbook or pencil brushes for draft lines, then switch to hair, manga, or line-focused tools as the illustration becomes more refined.
