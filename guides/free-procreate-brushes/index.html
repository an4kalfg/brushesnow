Curated brush picks

If you are searching for free Procreate brushes, start with packs that cover the basics well: texture, watercolor, sketching, patterns, and light rendering. This page focuses on broad-use sets that can help beginners explore different styles before building a more specialized brush library.

Best types of free Procreate brushes

The most useful free-style brush picks usually cover three needs: drawing, painting, and texture. A versatile starter collection often includes one sketch brush set, one watercolor or painterly set, and one texture or pattern pack.

What to download first

For a flexible setup, begin with a sketching pack, a texture pack, and a soft painting set. That combination gives you enough range for portraits, concept sketches, simple illustrations, and social media artwork.
