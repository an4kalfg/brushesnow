Ink Procreate Brushes for Clean Line Art

Curated ink and inking brush picks for line art, crosshatching, comic work, and black-and-white illustration.

Browse related categoryView all guides

Curated brush picks

Ink Procreate brushes are ideal when you need crisp edges, confident lines, expressive black-and-white detail, or controlled hatching. This guide covers inking-friendly packs for illustration, comics, and line-based character work.

What to look for in inking brushes

The best inking brushes feel stable at different sizes, stay readable when zoomed out, and still carry character when you vary pressure. Crosshatching and paper texture can add even more life to monochrome work.

How ink brushes fit with other tools

Ink brushes pair especially well with sketch brushes and manga texture tools. Sketch with soft pencil first, then refine with a cleaner inking brush and add hatching or screentone afterward.
