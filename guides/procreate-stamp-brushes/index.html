Curated brush picks

Procreate stamp brushes can save a huge amount of time when you need repeatable shapes, pose references, scene elements, or decorative motifs. This guide focuses on stamp-based tools that help with composition, lighting, planning, and stylized illustration.

When stamp brushes are worth using

Stamp brushes are especially useful when you build scenes, block in pose ideas, or repeat decorative elements. They speed up ideation and help you test layouts before you refine the final piece.

Good stamp brush workflows

Use stamp brushes for the rough structure first, then paint or ink over the result. This keeps the artwork feeling custom while still giving you the speed advantage of prebuilt shapes and guides.
