Sketch Procreate Brushes for Drawing and Ideation

A focused guide to sketch-friendly Procreate brushes for rough concepts, everyday drawing, and loose line work.

Browse related categoryView all guides

Curated brush picks

Sketch Procreate brushes are the foundation of a flexible drawing workflow. They help with gesture drawing, ideation, rough concepts, and early composition before you move to line art or rendering. This page focuses on pencil-like, paper-like, and concept-friendly brush sets.

Why sketch brushes matter

Good sketch brushes make it easier to think visually. They should feel responsive, readable at low opacity, and comfortable for quick iteration without making every rough line feel too polished too early.

Sketch brush setup tips

Try keeping one soft pencil brush, one textured sketch brush, and one cleaner line tool in your main Procreate set. That gives you quick control over rough construction, detail notes, and tighter drafts.
