Texture Procreate Brushes for Realistic Detail

Brush packs for grain, paper feel, fabric surfaces, and textured illustration workflows in Procreate.

Browse related categoryView all guides

Curated brush picks

Texture Procreate brushes help digital art feel less flat. They add grain, paper feel, fabric detail, rough surfaces, and subtle depth that can make illustrations feel more tactile. This guide collects texture-focused packs for backgrounds, clothing, painterly surfaces, and mixed-media style artwork.

Where texture brushes help most

Texture brushes are useful in backgrounds, clothing, paper simulations, and painterly overlays. Even a small amount of surface variation can make a clean digital piece feel more natural and dimensional.

How to layer texture in Procreate

Apply texture on separate layers so you can adjust opacity later. Start with large grain or paper texture, then add smaller detail selectively on focal areas such as clothes, hair, or the background.
