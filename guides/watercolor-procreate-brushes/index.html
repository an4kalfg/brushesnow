Curated brush picks

Watercolor Procreate brushes are ideal when you want soft edges, natural pigment flow, paper texture, and expressive painterly marks. This guide brings together watercolor-focused packs for botanical art, soft illustration, loose painting, and more controlled wet-wash rendering.

What makes a good watercolor brush set

The strongest watercolor sets usually combine wash brushes, edge texture, splatter, and paper feel. Together, those elements create the layered look that makes digital watercolor feel more convincing.

Watercolor brush ideas for beginners

If you are new to watercolor in Procreate, start with a soft wash brush, one splatter brush, and one texture brush. That small combination already gives you enough tools to practice floral work, character accents, and simple painted scenes.
